THE OTHER SIDE OF FAITH

Apostle Joshua Selman | Galatians 1:24, John 17:1

Introduction

Our walk with God and exploits in this Kingdom require the power of the Spirit. When the bride (the Church) and the Spirit become one, true manifestation happens.

Faith in the Scriptures

  • Hebrews 11: Faith is the substance of things hoped for and the evidence of things not seen.
  • John 1:3: All things were made by Him; without Him was not anything made that was made.
  • Hebrews 2:4: God also bearing witness with signs, wonders, and diverse miracles.
  • Romans 1:17: The just shall live by faith.
  • Habakkuk 2:4: The just shall live by his faith.
  • Galatians 3:7: They which are of faith, the same are the children of Abraham.

Men With Good Report in Heaven

  • Abel: Obtained witness that he was righteous by offering an excellent sacrifice.
  • Enoch: Had the testimony that he pleased God, and was translated without seeing death.
  • Noah: By faith built an ark to the saving of his household, becoming heir of righteousness.
  • Abraham: By faith obeyed and went out, looking for a city with foundations, whose builder and maker is God.
  • Job 42:10: The Lord turned the captivity of Job when he prayed for his friends, and gave him twice as much as before.
