MANIFESTING ABUNDANT LIFE

Apostle Joshua Selman

John 10:10, 1 Timothy 2

God’s Goals and Desires

  1. God’s greatest desire is that all men are saved through Jesus Christ to have salvation from sin and condemnation, and the salvation of our souls.
  2. God’s goal is that we live a victorious life. From creation, God gave man dominion and power over all things. We must believe, experience, and live out that exalted life.
  3. God’s purpose is that believers win the hearts and minds of men—revealing Jesus and making Him known to the world.

Three Gifts of God to Man When You Encounter Jesus

  • Forgiveness of sin
  • The Gift of Righteousness
  • The Life of God (Zoe) – Eternal life above the human life

When a believer gets saved, the next journey is learning wisdom, living by faith, and accessing the power of the Holy Ghost.

Three Vital Components to Manifest Zoe

  1. Wisdom of God: Jesus is the wisdom of God (Matthew 13:11).
