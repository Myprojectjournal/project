HUMILITY IN GOD

5-Minute Daily Humility Practice

Minute 1: Surrender

Close your eyes and say quietly: “Lord, I give You this day. Guide my thoughts, words, and actions.”

Picture: God stepping onto the throne in your heart-garden.

Minute 2: Gratitude

Think of 3 blessings God gave you today—even small ones like food, breath, or a smile.

Picture: Each blessing becomes a flower blooming in your garden.

Minute 3: Reflection

Ask yourself: “Where did I act out of pride today? Where did I follow God?”

Picture: Weeds being pulled wherever pride tried to grow.

Minute 4: Listening

Sit quietly and let God speak to your heart.

Picture: His light shining on the flowers, giving them strength.

Minute 5: Commitment

Pray: “Lord, help me stay humble, serve others, and keep You on the throne of my heart.”

Picture: A glowing sun filling your heart-garden with peace and joy.

How to Walk in Humility Daily

  • Surrender your day: Begin with prayer—invite God to lead, not pride.
  • Notice small blessings: Thank God for food, breath, friends, even struggles.
  • Listen more, speak less: Pause and ask, “Lord, what would You have me do?”
  • Confess mistakes quickly: Pride hides; humility brings sin to God’s light.
  • Serve others: Help someone without seeking praise.
  • Reflect at night: Thank God for victories, and ask strength for tomorrow.
Humility is not weakness—it is strength under God’s control. A humble heart is a throne where God always reigns.
