Psalm 71:20 · Zechariah 12:9

• THERE SHALL BE NO LOSS

Key scriptures: Psalm 71:20, Zechariah 12:9

Prayer does four key things

  1. Prayer activates the ministry of angels.
  2. Prayer allows you to exercise your will and the power of choice — to align your will with God’s will.
  3. Prayer allows you to receive counsel from the Holy Spirit and receive clear direction.
  4. Prayer activates discernment, helping us escape satanic traps and deceptions.

What does it mean to lose?

  1. To fail to win: the absence of victory is loss.
  2. To be unable to find: losing the ability to locate or recover what you once had.
  3. To stop being a possessor: having what should belong to you taken away — by accident or by design.

Six reasons why believers experience loss

Believers face loss for many reasons — understanding them helps us guard and recover:

  1. Lack of discernment — (see Hebrews 2:1; Isaiah 1:3; Ezekiel 12:1-2). Without discernment we miss spiritual cues and warnings.
  2. Carelessness — (Hebrews 2:2; Judges 11:13-35). Negligence opens doors to loss.
  3. Ignorance of spiritual laws — There are kingdom laws that guide life and destiny; do not assume things happen without cause.
  4. Abuse and misuse — as illustrated in the parable of the talents: mismanaging gifts/resources leads to loss.
  5. Tests and trials — (see Job chapters). Life’s trials refine; be careful with misplaced compassion that drains you. Victory is a product of light and endurance.
  6. Demonic and satanic attack — the enemy seeks to steal, kill and destroy; spiritual warfare can cost us if unguarded.
"Victory is a product of light. When revelation and obedience meet, loss is reversed and recovery begins."
Practical guardrails: cultivate discernment through prayer and Scripture, stop carelessness, learn kingdom laws, steward your gifts, endure tests with patience, and pursue spiritual protection.
Father, grant us discerning eyes to see the schemes of the enemy and the wisdom to avoid carelessness.
Lord, teach us Your laws for life and destiny; help us steward every gift and resource well.
Holy Spirit, empower us to persevere through tests and to turn trials into testimony.
