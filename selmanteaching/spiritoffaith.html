SPIRIT OF FAITH

God’s Glory Through His Saints

God is glorified to the degree His wisdom, life, power, and grace are made manifest in His saints. You must believe that your rising, success, and excellence brings glory to God.

“You have not chosen Me, but I have chosen you and ordained you, that you should go and bring forth fruit, and that your fruit should remain.”

A Great God, A Strong People

  • A great God cannot create a weak people—it would misrepresent Him.
  • To be blessed means to be empowered.
  • Prosperity means to move forward and advance.
  • God has blessed us with all spiritual blessings in heavenly places.
