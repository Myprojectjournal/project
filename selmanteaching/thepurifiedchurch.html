THE PURIFIED CHURCH

Christ’s Return

Jesus Christ is coming back. It is the assignment of the five-fold ministry to build and mature the body as we await His coming.

The way the Lord purifies His bride is by commending the Church and also correcting the areas that need adjustment.

Balance in Building: To build mature believers, we must be lavish in both commendation and correction. - Only commending = weak people. - Only correcting = discouraged people.

7 Sins That Must Be Stopped

1. Sexual Immorality & Related Perversions

The desire for intimacy is not demonic. It is God-given, but must be expressed within God’s conditions. Lust and immorality pervert this desire to man’s destruction.

5 Steps to Overcome:

  • Admit it honestly.
  • Set aside time for personal retreat (Psalm 51).
  • Where it persists, seek help.
  • Create rules and boundaries in life.
  • Connect to a larger family of believers.

2. Lust for Money & Material Things

Materialism is not about owning things, but when things own you. God wants to bless us, but not with hearts attached to money.

First law of wealth: absolute surrender. Until you die to self and fully yield to God, you are only bargaining with Him.

3. Witchcraft & Extra-Biblical Practices

We must be delivered from manipulation, sorcery, and false practices (Ezekiel 13).

Three Types of People in the Kingdom:

  • Pure breeds: Pure in heart, love God, avoid evil.
  • Innocent but misled: Sincere but diverted by bad mentorship and wrong friends.
  • From inception not of God: Evil at heart, committed to deception and demonic works.

The cure to witchcraft and manipulation is the fear of God.

4. Pride, Vain Glory & Self-Centeredness

Pride downplays others and makes everything about “me.” God condemns pride and self-centeredness.

John said: “A man can receive nothing, except it be given him from heaven… He must increase, but I must decrease.”

There is a difference between simplicity and humility. One can be simple yet still prideful.

5. The Sin of the Tongue

Keep your tongue from evil and lies. The Lord hates these six things (Proverbs 6):

  • A proud look
  • A lying tongue
  • Hands that shed innocent blood
  • A heart devising wicked imaginations
  • Feet swift to mischief
  • A false witness speaking lies
  • One who sows discord among brethren

6. Competition & Unhealthy Comparison

Competition is a dangerous spirit. The Bible says those who compare themselves with others are not wise.

If we think we are the only ones making impact, we deceive ourselves and insult the Holy Spirit who works through many bodies in Christ.

7. The Sin of Imbalance

Extremes and imbalance in doctrine or practice harm the body of Christ. Truth must always be balanced with love, humility, and wisdom.

The purified Church is the bride prepared for Christ’s return: holy, humble, balanced, and rooted in truth.
