ZOE LIFE 2 – THE MINISTRY LIFE

The Zoe Life grants us the possibility and capacity to live like God and function like God.

What does it mean to live like God?

It means to manifest the character of God even though we are trapped in human form.

There is a difference between a Living Soul and a Life-Giving Spirit. A living soul only enjoys the life given to him; he cannot transmit it to anybody. But a life-giving spirit can extend what he carries. This is what Jesus meant when He said: “Out of your belly shall flow rivers of living water.”

If you want to understand the character of God, study demon possession. The first ability given to man is power to become, not just power against. Our spirit-to-spirit salvation must sink into the soulical realm (mind, will, emotions). The body is just an instrument of execution—it obeys whatever “software” is written in the soul.

The Bible says: “You shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free.” Sometimes it is not a spirit holding you captive but your own understanding.

What does it mean to manifest and live like Christ?

  • It means to rise above sin and the flesh.
  • It means to manifest the fruit of the Spirit.

Seven things Jesus delivered us from:

  1. Sin
  2. Flesh
  3. World
  4. Death
  5. Grave

The sin problem is solved immediately after salvation, but the flesh problem is not solved immediately. Freedom is not endurance—true liberty comes by understanding the law of liberty.

The First Laws to Live Like Christ

  1. You must believe that the power to overcome sin and flesh is already within you.
  2. Romans 6:11 – “Reckon yourselves dead to sin but alive to God in Christ Jesus.”
  3. Hold fast your profession of faith regardless of circumstances.
  4. Yield your members (body) to decisions that reflect Christ-like living. The power to honor decisions comes from God.
  5. Engage in spiritual activities that strengthen your spirit:
    • Prayer
    • Listening to the Word
    • Church fellowship and activities

The Fruit of the Holy Spirit

  • Love: When you don’t have an answer, say “God is greater.”
  • Comfort: The Holy Spirit is our comforter.
  • Wisdom: Extraordinary wisdom to function like God.
  • Creativity & Innovation: Always say “God show me the way,” because there is always a way.
  • Excellence: To reflect God’s order and beauty.
  • Wealth: Wealth is a response to value, not love of money. True servants of God should not beg.
  • Mighty Works: The fruit of wisdom manifested in power.
  • Life: Every spiritual man functioning like God becomes a life-giving spirit.
